Links for K-12 Tech Integration

So much is changing, and teachers are under increased pressure to integrate technology in lessons. But, as teachers we don't just want to integrate technology because we're told to, we want to use technology in a way that helps students develop critical thinking and problem solving skills. It can be intimidating when it feels like the students know more than we do about technology, but we all have to start somewhere! Be patient with yourself, and try out some of the recommended apps and sites. Using technology in the classroom has a great potential to increase student engagement and help them develop 21st century skills. Here's a few resources to help get you started!
